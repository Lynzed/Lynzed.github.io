Refund Policy

We strive to provide our customers with the best possible service and products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we offer a refund policy under the following terms and conditions.

Limited Edition, Sales and Last Stock Items

Please note that Limited Edition, Sales and Last Stock Items are final sale and cannot be returned.

Time Frame for Returns

Our refund policy lasts 72 hours from the date of purchase. If 72 hours have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund.

Gift Card Payments

Gift card payments are not refundable.

Requirements for Returns

To complete your return, we require a receipt or proof of purchase, the license key, payment date & time, payment method & name & e-mail used (email part doesn't apply for gift card payments).

Software with Blue or Red Status

Please note that software with Blue or Red Status cannot be returned.

Processing of Refunds

Once your return is received and inspected, we will send you a message to notify you that we have received your returned item. We will also notify you of the approval or rejection of your refund.

If your refund is approved, we will process it within 14 business days. Refunds on credit cards may take 1-2 billing cycles to appear on your credit card statement.

Non-Refundable Items

Please note that shipping costs, fees, and any other related charges are non-refundable.

Fees

Refund fees including handling and service fees of total up to 40% may apply.

Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please contact us through Modmail.
